Frequently Asked Questions

What if the seller refuses the audit? expand_more
If a seller refuses an audit, we strongly recommend finding a different vehicle. Transparency is the cornerstone of a safe purchase. A refusal often indicates undisclosed issues. GariAudit is here to ensure you never buy a lemon.
Is the audit fee refundable? expand_more
Fees are refundable up to 24 hours before the scheduled audit. If the auditor has already begun the journey to the vehicle location, a partial travel fee may apply. Contact our support team for specific cases.
How accurate is the OBD scan? expand_more
Our OBD-II scans use industrial-grade diagnostic tools that interface directly with the vehicle's ECU. We can detect hidden error codes, mileage discrepancies, and emissions issues that standard consumer-grade scanners often miss.
Can I request a specific auditor? expand_more
Yes, you can browse auditor profiles and request specific individuals based on their ratings and expertise in certain vehicle brands.
