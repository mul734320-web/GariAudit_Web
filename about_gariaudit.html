Our Story

GariAudit was born out of personal frustration. Our founders, regular car enthusiasts, spent months scrolling through FB Marketplace and Bikroy, only to find that every "mint condition" car hidden serious mechanical flaws.

In a market where trust was a rare commodity, we saw an opportunity to bridge the gap. We realized that for the Bangladeshi car market to mature, it needed a neutral, expert third party that spoke only the language of data.

What started as a small team of three mechanics in a rented garage has now evolved into Bangladesh's most trusted vehicle inspection network.
